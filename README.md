**Amazon Sales & Product ,Customer Analytics Dashboard **

Power BI dashboards analyzing Amazon order and customer data — covering 100,000
orders, 43,233 customers, and ₹91.83M in revenue across five countries (2020–2024).

📁 Dataset
- 100,000 orders | 43,233 unique customers | 300,140 units sold
- 20 fields: order details, customer info, product/category, pricing, discount,
  tax, shipping cost, payment method, order status, and location
- Time period: January 2020 – December 2024

**🔎 Key Insights**

**Revenue & Orders**
- Total revenue: ₹91.83M | Average revenue per order: ₹918.26
- Top 5 products by revenue: Memory Card 128GB, LED Desk Lamp, Mechanical
  Keyboard, Electric Kettle, and Smartwatch (each ₹1.90M–1.94M)
- Product categories are evenly distributed — no single category dominates,
  suggesting a diversified catalog strategy

**Geography**
- United States leads by revenue (₹64.3M, ~70% of total), followed by India
  (₹13.9M), Canada (₹5.3M), UK (₹4.5M), and Australia (₹3.8M)

**Customer Behavior**
- Average of 2.31 orders per customer, ₹2.12K average customer spend
- Credit Card is the dominant payment method (35% of orders), followed by
  Debit Card (20%) and UPI (15%)

**Order Fulfillment**
- 74.63% of orders successfully delivered
- 15.19% still in Shipped status; combined Returned + Cancelled rate is ~6.1%

**🛠️ Tools & Technologies**
- **Power BI** — dashboard design, DAX measures, interactive filtering, geo mapping
- **Microsoft Excel** — data cleaning, transformation, and preparation
